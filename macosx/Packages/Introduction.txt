Source for HDF4, HDF5, NetCDF4, HDF-EOS2, Libjpeg, and zlib: /Applications/Mtk_thirdparty

Install of HDF4, HDF5, NetCDF4, HDF-EOS2, Libjpeg, and zlib: /usr/local/hdfeoslibs

Install of MISR Toolkit: /Applications/Mtk-1.5.1
